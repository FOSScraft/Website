About FOSScraft

A community-driven Minecraft launcher

Our Mission

FOSScraft is a free and open-source Minecraft launcher built to provide players with a modern, intuitive way to manage their Minecraft installations. We're committed to providing a community-driven, transparent alternative to proprietary launchers.

We believe in transparency, freedom, and community. Our code is open for anyone to review, modify, and improve. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality while respecting the rights and privacy of our users.

Building & Customization

FOSScraft is open for anyone to build and customize. When building for your distribution or creating custom builds, please:

  • Update FOSScraft's API keys in CMakeLists.txt to your own or set them to empty strings
  • For distribution packages, set the Launcher_BUILD_PLATFORM to a slug representing your distribution (e.g., archlinux, fedora)

If you have any questions about building FOSScraft, please open an issue and ask us.
