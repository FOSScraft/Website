About FOSScraft

Our Mission

FOSScraft is a free and open-source Minecraft launcher built to provide players with a modern, intuitive way to manage their Minecraft installations. Originally based on Prism Launcher, FOSScraft continues the mission of providing a community-driven, transparent alternative to proprietary launchers.
